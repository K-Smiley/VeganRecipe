The Best Tofu Scramble

If you miss scrambled eggs, this oneâ€™s for you. Just 7 ingredients, 10 minutes and 1 pan required!

Ingredients

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon black salt
  • 100g spinach
  • pinch of turmeric to colour
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • olive oil

Instructions

  • Add a tablespoon of olive oil to a pan and warm it over medium heat
  • Saute chopped onions until soft
  • Next, crumble tofu in pan and fry for 3-4 mins
  • Then, add the nutritional yeast, salt, turmeric and garlic powder and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring
  • Lastly, stir in spinach until wilted
  • Serve immediately on toast

Why not try serving this with avocado and hot sauce? Or with thinly sliced red peppers and small chopped broccoli? Play around with it and get creative!
